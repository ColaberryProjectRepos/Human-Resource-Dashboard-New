Step 4 – Refresh Date

Creating Refresh Date Table


Steps :

1. Open Power BI Desktop and navigate to the ribbon.

2. Click on "Transform data."

3. In the Transform data window, click on the "New Source" button.

4. Select "Blank query" from the list and click on "Connect."

5. In the Properties, enter 'Refresh Date' in the Name box.

6. On the DAX formula bar, type '=DateTime.LocalNow().'

7. Click on the "Convert to Table" button.

8. When the refresh table appears, right-click on the column and choose "Rename" from the selection, to rename it as - “Date time”

9. Click on the "Close & Apply" button and save your changes.
